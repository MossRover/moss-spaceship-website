About MossRover

MossRover is a Synth, originally manufactured as an "affordable" boxing trainer prototype — a project that turned out to be a failure. He was on the verge of being scrapped, but was rescued and repaired by Axu. He now works as an Exoplanet Explorer and Biologist.

Moss is calm and a bit shy at first, but once he opens up he's energetic, fun and reliable. He loves nature, peaceful places, landscape photography and spending time with his friends.
